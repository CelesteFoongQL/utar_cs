  3. Practice before going for interview:

    • a. Understand background and culture of the company.

    • b. Prepare appropriate answer for the question that may be ask by the company during interview session.

Skills to Answer Phone Call From the Industry



  1. Always pay attention on your phone after submit the resume because the phone may call from the company.

  2. Be in a quiet place with a good phone connection during the interview.

  3. Speak slowly.

  4. Sip water periodically - it will help you speak clearly.

  5. Avoid chewing gum, eating or smoking.

  6. Answer with your name. Answering by saying "this is (your name)" so the caller knows he's reached the right number and doesn't have to ask for you.

  7. Listen carefully the incoming phone and record the caller's name or name of in charge person.

  8. Focus on the conversation. Stop whatever you might be doing and take a brief moment to prepare. Wear the face you want to project before picking up the phone. It makes a difference: whether you're smiling, frowning, or bored to tears, your caller will hear that in your tone. Smile - it makes your voice sound friendly

  9. When you answer the phone, be warm and enthusiastic. Your voice on the phone is sometimes the only impression of caller will get.

  10. Control your language when answering the phone. Don't use slang or buzzwords, such as 'cheers', 'uh-huh', 'mate', 'yeah' etc.

  11. Give short answers - talk long enough to answer the question but don't feel you need to fill every silence. An interview should be a dialogue rather than a monologue.

  12. Enunciate clearly, keep your voice volume moderate, and speak slowly and clearly when answering the phone, so your caller can understand you easily.

  13. Take phone messages completely and accurately. If there's something you don't listen clearly, ask the caller to repeat it for you. Then make sure gets the correct message.

  14. Acknowledge your understanding of the issue at hand, repeating back the main points if necessary.

  15. End the call with a positive statement. For example, thank the person for contacting you.
