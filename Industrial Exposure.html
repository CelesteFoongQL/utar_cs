What to Prepare?


  1. Wear formal attire.

  2. Prepare extra resume for emergency purposes.

  3. Practice before going for interview:

    1. Understand background and culture of the company.

    2. Prepare appropriate answers for questions that may be asked during interview session.

Skills to Answer Phone Call From the Industry


  1. Always be ready to answer your phone after submitting the resume.

  2. Be in a quiet place with good phone connection during the conversation.

  3. Speak slowly.

  4. Sip water periodically - it helps you speak clearly.

  5. Avoid chewing gum, eating or smoking.

  6. Answer with your name. Answer by saying "this is (your name)" so the caller knows he reached the right person.

  7. Listen carefully to the incoming phone call and record the caller's name.

  8. Focus on the conversation. Stop whatever you might be doing and take a brief moment to prepare.

  9. Smile - it makes your voice sound friendly

  10. When you answer the phone, be warm and enthusiastic. Your voice on the phone is sometimes the only impression of the caller would get.

  11. Control your language when answering the phone. Don't use slang or buzzwords, such as 'uh-huh', 'mate' etc.

  12. Give short answers - An interview should be a dialogue rather than a monologue.

  13. Enunciate clearly, keep your voice volume moderate, and speak slowly and clearly so that your caller can understand you easily.

  14. Take phone messages accurately. If you missed out something, kindly ask the caller to repeat, then make sure you get the correct message.

  15. Acknowledge your understanding of the issue at hand, repeating back the main points if necessary.

  16. End the call with a positive statement. Say "Thank You".
